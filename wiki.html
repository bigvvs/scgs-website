General Commands

Essential commands available on our server:

  • Open Inventory (Pockets): - TAB :F2
  • Open Car Inventory: - K
  • Emotes Menu: - F3
  • Sitting In Chair: - /e sitchair or third eye the chair
  • Carry someone or stop carrying someone: - /carry
  • Right Control : - crawl
  • To point : - B
  • To go to a postal : - /postal [postal number]
  • third eye : - ALT
  • To play music in car : - /radiocar and post the link
  • To reloadskin : - /reloadskin
  • To report : - /report
  • Hands Up : - x or /handsup
